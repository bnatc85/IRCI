IRCI Dials

Valuation

What it is: Percentile of your EV/EBITDA vs. peers (lower multiple → stronger score).

High: You’re attractively valued vs. peers → protect consistency in guidance & disclosure.

Mid: Mixed signals → sharpen narrative and KPIs tied to cash conversion.

Low: Rich multiple or weak earnings quality → increase fundamentals transparency, host deep-dives.

Liquidity

What it is: Turnover / spreads rolled to quarter; higher turnover & tighter spreads → higher score.

High: Smooth trading; maintain predictable newsflow.

Mid: Patchy depth; improve calendarization & broker engagement.

Low: Friction; expand coverage days, target liquidity providers, refine float communications.

Coverage

What it is: Timeliness (10-Q/10-K), 8-K cadence, and sell-side attention.

High: Good cadence; keep pre-announced dates and post-call materials.

Mid: Inconsistent; lock a disclosure calendar and add analyst roundtables.

Low: Sparse; accelerate filings, add primers/teach-ins, broaden broker touchpoints.

Trust (Media/Sentiment)

What it is: Event calmness, baseline calmness, and media tone.

High: Stable residuals & constructive tone; keep crisp narratives.

Mid: Choppy; tighten message discipline, address FAQs proactively.

Low: Volatile/negative; issue clarifications, publish metrics, increase access to leadership.
