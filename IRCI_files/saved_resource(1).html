How IRCI Works

  1. Ingest: Prices, Fama-French factors (or CAPM fallback), SEC events, news tone.
  2. Score dials: Each dial → 0–100 percentile vs. peer-set/quarter.
  3. Composite: Weighted average with missing-dial re-normalization.

Valuation% = 100 × rank_pct(− EV/EBITDA)
Liquidity% = 100 × rank_pct(− Spread_bps, + Turnover)
Coverage%  = 100 × rank_pct(− Days_to_10Q/10K, − 8-K spikes)
Trust%     = 100 × rank_pct( w1·EventCalm + w2·BaselineCalm + w3·MediaTone )

Composite% = (Σ w_dial · Dial%) / (Σ w_dial for available dials)
